About the Portal

GreenQuest Punjab is a gamified learning platform created by the Government of Punjab to inspire schools, students, and teachers to actively participate in environmental education.

Students can complete lessons, take quizzes, and participate in eco-challenges to earn Eco-Points, badges, and recognition. Teachers can create and verify challenges, track class progress, and motivate learners.

This portal helps measure collective environmental impact across Punjab’s schools, driving awareness and sustainable action for a greener future.
